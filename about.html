About Us

Who We Are

Naloxone Advocates Plymouth CIC is a community-led organisation dedicated to reducing drug-related deaths through education, training, and direct action.

Peer-led • Lived experience
Compassion • Dignity • Every life matters

What we do

  • Free naloxone training and awareness sessions
  • Peer-to-peer support and community outreach
  • Sharing practical harm reduction information

Our approach

We’re non-judgemental, trauma-informed and focused on safety. We work alongside local services and the community to make overdose response knowledge widely available.

If you’re a service, workplace, community group, or an individual who wants training or naloxone support, we’ll help you figure out the best option.

Talk to us

Why we exist

Overdose is preventable. Naloxone saves lives, and training builds confidence. Our work is about making life-saving skills and tools accessible across Plymouth & Devon.

Emergency: always call 999 first. Naloxone is a temporary measure — professional medical help is essential.
