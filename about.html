About Us

We’re a community-led organisation focused on reducing drug-related deaths through naloxone training, harm reduction and peer support.

What we do

  • Free naloxone training and awareness sessions
  • Peer-to-peer support and community outreach
  • Sharing practical harm reduction information

Our approach

Non-judgemental, trauma-informed, and focused on safety. We work alongside local services and the community to make overdose response knowledge widely available.
